Focus Tips
💡
Eliminate Distractions

Put your phone in another room and close non-essential browser tabs before starting a deep work block.

🏃
Active Breaks

During short breaks, stand up and stretch. Avoid checking social media; it doesn't actually reset your brain.

✦
Single-Tasking

Focus on one specific sub-goal per Pomodoro. Multitasking reduces academic performance by up to 40%.
